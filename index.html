Hi! I’m an experimental physicist and systems engineer. Currently, I work for Facebook in the greater Los Angeles area on free-space laser communication and related technologies to make the Internet available to the billions of people without it. Previously, I researched atoms and molecules in vapor cells or at ultracold temperatures to make better atomic clocks and more precise measurements. I’m incurably curious, and love finding and solving mysteries.

Free access to my publications from Facebook, a postdoc in the ZLab at Columbia, and graduate school at Princeton is available here. A few technical(-ish) notes for people with similar interests are available here.
